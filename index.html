Our Story

About VASTRA

Vastra is a modern streetwear and fashion brand focused on premium quality oversized t-shirts, custom graphic designs, anime collections, mythology-inspired apparel, and trend-driven fashion. Every design is crafted to help individuals express their personality through clothing.

Founded with the belief that fashion should be a canvas for self-expression, VASTRA blends cultural narratives with contemporary streetwear aesthetics to create pieces that are as unique as the people who wear them.
